Vitamin D Rapid Test

Test your Vitamin D levels in 15-minutes quickly and conveniently from home. Health Canada approved.

The Vitamin D Rapid Test Kit provides an easy way to assess your Vitamin D (25-hydroxyvitamin D) status using a small blood sample. Results are available within 15 minutes, helping you take proactive steps toward better health.

Key Features

  • Simple finger-prick blood
  • Results available in 15 minutes
  • Results: Deficient, Insufficient, Sufficient
  • Ships across Canada

What's Included

  • 1 Rapid Test Cassette
  • 1 Sterile Lancet
  • 1 Capillary Dropper
  • Buffer Solution
  • Easy-to-follow Instructions
